# App Branding & Icon Guidelines

## App Icon

### Required Sizes
The app icon must be provided in the following sizes for iOS:

- **1024x1024** - App Store
- **180x180** - iPhone @3x
- **120x120** - iPhone @2x
- **167x167** - iPad Pro @2x
- **152x152** - iPad @2x
- **76x76** - iPad @1x
- **60x60** - iPhone Spotlight @3x
- **40x40** - iPhone Spotlight @2x

### Design Guidelines

**Style:**
- Simple, bold, and high contrast
- Works well at small sizes (avoid fine details)
- Recognizable in both light and dark modes
- No text or wordmarks (icon should be symbolic)

**Suggested Visual:**
- Primary element: Upward trending line chart or forecast curve
- Color palette: Orange accent (`#FA8A24`) on dark background
- Shape: Consider circular or rounded square base
- Style: Modern, minimal, professional

**Format Requirements:**
- PNG format with alpha channel
- sRGB color space
- No transparency in the corners (for iOS home screen)
- Export at 1024x1024 first, then scale down

### Export Settings
- Use bicubic interpolation when scaling
- Save for web/apps (optimized)
- Maintain sharp edges on geometric shapes
- Test visibility on both light and dark wallpapers
